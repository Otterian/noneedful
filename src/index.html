Please Stop Saying "Needful"

"Please do the needful"

Instead, be specific about what you need:

  • "Please review and approve this document"
  • "Please process this request"
  • "Please complete these specific tasks"

The phrase "do the needful" is vague and can lead to:

  • Confusion about responsibilities
  • Miscommunication
  • Unnecessary follow-up questions
  • Delayed actions
